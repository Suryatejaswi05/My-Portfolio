Tackling Fake News

In the age of rampant misinformation, this project uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to analyze news articles and predict their authenticity. It helps users distinguish between factual reporting and fake news.

Key Features:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze text content.
  • Machine learning model trained on a large dataset of real and fake news articles.
  • Provides a probability score of an article being fake.
  • Browser extension prototype to check news in real-time.
